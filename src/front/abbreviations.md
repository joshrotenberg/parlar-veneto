## Orthographic Conventions

| Symbol | Represents | Notes |
|--------|------------|-------|
| ł | "evanescent L" | Velarized, often silent or /w/-like |
| x | voiced /z/ | As in *xe* (is), *xente* (people) |
| s- | voiceless /s/ | Word-initial before vowel |
| -s- | voiced /z/ | Intervocalic |
| ' | elision | *ła* > *l'aqua* |
