# Appendix C: Glossary

This glossary collects key Venetian words appearing throughout the grammar, organized alphabetically. For each entry: Venetian term, part of speech, English translation, and notes where relevant.

## A

**acqua** *n.f.* - water
- *acqua alta* - high water (Venice flooding)

**anca** *adv.* - also, too (Italian: *anche*)

**andar / ndar** *v.* - to go
- *Ndemo!* - Let's go!

**ancó / oncó** *adv.* - today (Italian: *oggi*)

**articiochi** *n.m.pl.* - artichokes

**aver / gavér** *v.* - to have

## B

**bacaro** *n.m.* - traditional Venetian wine bar

**baccalà** *n.m.* - salt cod
- *baccalà mantecà* - creamed salt cod

**basta** *interj.* - enough, stop

**beo / beło** *adj.* - beautiful, handsome

**bira** *n.f.* - beer

**bisato** *n.m.* - eel

**bon** *adj.* - good

## C

**cafè** *n.m.* - coffee

**can** *n.m.* - dog

**caparossołi** *n.m.pl.* - clams (also: *bevarasse*)

**casa** *n.f.* - house, home

**ciao** *interj.* - hello, goodbye (originally Venetian, from *s-ciào vostro*)

**cicheto** *n.m.* - small snack, Venetian tapa

**ciò** *interj.* - hey, listen (filler particle, very Venetian)

**ciucio / ciuco** *adj.* - drunk

**compare** *n.m.* - buddy, pal

**cossa** *pron.* - what (Italian: *cosa*)

## D

**daghe** *interj.* - go for it! give it!

**dai** *interj.* - come on!

**desso / adesso** *adv.* - now

**doman** *adv.* - tomorrow

**dona** *n.f.* - woman

**dormir** *v.* - to sleep

**dove** *adv.* - where

## E

**èsser** *v.* - to be

## F

**fame** *n.f.* - hunger
- *Go fame.* - I'm hungry.

**far** *v.* - to do, to make
- *Cossa te feto?* - What are you doing?

**fiol / fiola** *n.* - son / daughter

**folpo** *n.m.* - octopus

**forsa** *interj.* - come on! strength!

**fradel / fradeo** *n.m.* - brother (also: bro, friend)

**freschin** *n.m.* - musty smell

**fritołe** *n.f.pl.* - carnival fritters

## G

**gato** *n.m.* - cat

**gavér** *v.* - to have (see *aver*)

**ghe** *pron./adv.* - to him/her/them; there
- *Ghe xe...* - There is/are...

**gnanca** *adv.* - not even

**gnente** *pron.* - nothing

**goto** *n.m.* - glass (drinking glass)

**grando** *adj.* - big, large

**granso** *n.m.* - crab

**grassie** *interj.* - thanks

## I

**inbriago** *adj.* - drunk

## L

**laorar** *v.* - to work (Italian: *lavorare*)

**leto** *n.m.* - bed

**libro** *n.m.* - book

**luni** *n.m.* - Monday

## M

**magnar** *v.* - to eat

**mai** *adv.* - never

**mare** *n.f.* - mother

**marì** *n.m.* - husband

**miga** *adv.* - at all (emphatic negation)
- *No go miga capìo.* - I didn't understand at all.

**moeche** *n.f.pl.* - soft-shell crabs

**mona** *n.* - fool, idiot (vulgar origin, very common)

**moroso / morosa** *n.* - boyfriend / girlfriend

**mujér** *n.f.* - wife

## N

**Nadae** *n.m.* - Christmas (Italian: *Natale*)

**ndemo** *interj.* - let's go!

**nevodo / nevoda** *n.* - nephew/niece, grandchild

**nissun** *pron.* - nobody

**no** *adv.* - no, not

**nono / nona** *n.* - grandfather / grandmother

**note** *n.f.* - night

## O

**ocio** *interj.* - watch out! (lit. "eye")

**ombra** *n.f.* - glass of wine (lit. "shadow")
- *Ndemo par un'ombra?* - Shall we go for a glass?

**omo** *n.m.* - man

**ostia** *interj.* - mild expletive (lit. "communion wafer")

## P

**pan** *n.m.* - bread

**parché** *conj.* - because, why

**pare** *n.m.* - father

**parlar** *v.* - to speak

**partir** *v.* - to leave

**peoci** *n.m.pl.* - mussels

**pesse** *n.m.* - fish

**piaser** *n.m.* - pleasure
- *par piaser* - please

**polenta** *n.f.* - polenta

**pomi** *n.m.pl.* - tomatoes (lit. "apples")

**proseco** *n.m.* - prosecco (note: single 'c')

**punciotto** *n.m.* - traditional Venetian snack

**puteo / putea** *n.* - child (boy/girl)

## Q

**quando** *adv.* - when
- *quando che* - when (with Venetian *che* insertion)

**queło** *pron.* - that

## R

**radicchio** *n.m.* - radicchio

**risi e bisi** *n.m.pl.* - rice and peas (spring dish)

## S

**salute** *interj.* - cheers! health!

**sarde in saor** *n.f.pl.* - sardines in sweet-sour marinade

**schei** *n.m.pl.* - money

**schie** *n.f.pl.* - tiny gray lagoon shrimp

**scolta** *interj.* - listen!

**sepa** *n.f.* - cuttlefish

**sgroppino** *n.m.* - lemon sorbet + vodka + prosecco

**sidià** *adj.* - annoyed, fed up

**soreła** *n.f.* - sister

**sparasi** *n.m.pl.* - asparagus

**speta** *interj.* - wait!

**spriss** *n.m.* - spritz (Venetian pronunciation)

**strassa** *adj.* - exhausted

## T

**tasi** *interj.* - shut up! be quiet!

**tol** *interj.* - take! here! (imperative of *tòr*)

**tosa** *n.f.* - girl

**toso** *n.m.* - boy, guy

**tramezzini** *n.m.pl.* - triangular sandwiches

## V

**varda** *interj.* - look! (imperative of *vardar*)

**vardar** *v.* - to look, to watch (Italian: *guardare*)

**vecio** *n.m./adj.* - old man; old; also term of endearment ("buddy")

**véder** *v.* - to see

**vegnir** *v.* - to come

**veneto** *n.m./adj.* - Venetian (the language/person/thing)

**vin** *n.m.* - wine

**voler** *v.* - to want

## X

**xe** *v.* - is, are (3rd person of *èsser*)
- *Ghe xe* - There is/are
- *Come xeła?* - How's it going?

**xòbia** *n.f.* - Thursday

**xorno** *n.m.* - day (Italian: *giorno*)

## Z

**xogar** *v.* - to play (Italian: *giocare*)

## Grammatical Terms

**clitic** - unstressed pronoun that attaches to the verb (*te*, *el*, *la*, *i*, *le*)

**complementizer** - *che* inserted after conjunctions (*quando che*, *dove che*)

**evanescent L** - the Venetian *ł*, a velarized or weakened L sound

**ghe xe** - existential construction "there is/are"

**miga** - emphatic negation particle

**passato prossimo** - compound past tense (*go magnà*)

**terraferma** - the Venetian mainland (Mestre, etc.)
